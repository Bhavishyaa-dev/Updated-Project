Frequently Asked Questions

How do I sign up for Streamix?

To sign up for Streamix, click the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill in the required information.

How can I reset my password?

If you forgot your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription by going to your account settings and selecting the "Cancel Subscription" option.
