Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner and follow the registration process. You'll need to provide your email address and create a password.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email address, and follow the instructions sent to your email.

How do I add movies to my watchlist?

Click the "+" icon on any movie card or the "Add to List" button on the movie details page.

How do I change my subscription plan?

Go to your account settings, select "Subscription", and choose your desired plan.
